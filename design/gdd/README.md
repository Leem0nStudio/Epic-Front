## Design Principles
1. **Idle-Friendly**: Core loop supports both active play and idle progression
2. **Fair Monetization**: FTP players can progress; payers progress faster
3. **Strategic Depth**: Job evolution and team composition matter
4. **Pacing**: Energy system encourages 15-30 min play sessions
5. **Clear Progression**: Players always have a goal (next stage, next evolution)

## Key Metrics
- **Session Length**: 15-30 minutes (energy depletes)
- **Time to Max Level**: ~3-6 months for active players
- **Gacha Spend**: $0-50/month FTP, $10-100/month mild spenders
- **Retention**: Daily rewards + energy refills encourage daily login
