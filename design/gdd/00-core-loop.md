# Core Loop - Epic RPG

## Overview
Epic RPG is a web-based RPG with idle/auto-battle mechanics. Players collect units, equip them with gear, and battle through campaign stages to progress.

## Primary Loop
1. **Battle** → Select stage, deploy party, watch auto-battle
2. **Reward** → Gain currency, EXP, materials
3. **Upgrade** → Level up units, change jobs, equip better gear
4. **Progress** → Unlock harder stages, expand party

## Secondary Loops
- **Gacha**: Spend premium currency to recruit new units, get weapons/cards
- **Evolution**: Meet requirements (level, cost, materials) to evolve jobs
- **Daily Rewards**: Login streak for bonus resources

## Session Flow
1. Player logs in → sees daily rewards, energy status
2. Checks available stages (energy permitting)
3. Battles stage → gets rewards
4. Upgrades units/gear with rewards
5. Repeats until energy depleted or done playing
6. Can use gems to refill energy or pull gacha

## Key Metrics
- **Energy regen**: 1 per 4 minutes (max 30)
- **Stage clear time**: ~30 seconds to 2 minutes
- **Level progression**: ~5-10 stages per level
- **Job evolution**: Tier 1 (Lv10), Tier 2 (Lv40), Tier 3 (Lv70), Tier 4 (Lv90)
